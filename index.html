About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer based in Lisbon, available for remote freelance contracts worldwide. With over 11 years of industry experience, my core expertise lies in C++ gameplay programming and AI architecture.

I’ve often been described as a “designer masquerading as a programmer”. That is to say, I care deeply about the feel of the game, not just the code behind it.

I started in the modding scene for Half-Life 2, and I pride myself on being a reliable, autonomous remote worker who fosters a friendly and collaborative environment (us modders in 2004 were doing that before it was mainstream).

Whether leading a team or working solo, I focus on clear communication and building systems that empower designers and artists.

Currently working with PLAYERUNKNOWN Productions on next-generation experiences.
